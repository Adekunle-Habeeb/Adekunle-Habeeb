The software also identifies critical path, which further aids in project management. Additionally, I am a member of The AI Research team at Nigeria Information Technology Development Agency (NITDA) Hub, University of Lagos, where we are currently working on a food price prediction model, which offers a great contribution to food and agriculture sectors. I remain committed to creating scalable and effective solutions in servitude of mankind and contribution to the entire tech-community at large.
